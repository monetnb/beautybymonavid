Policies

Booking Policy:

You may pay with your preferred form of payment on the day of your scheduled appointment. All appointments are booked directly through me. To book an appointment you may text me at (949)-423-4646, direct message me on instagram, or use the "book now" button on the website. No refunds will be granted for any lash services.

Cancellation/Reschedule Policy:

A 48 hour notice is required so I can adjust my schedule and accommodate other clients. Lashes By Mona reserves the right to charge a cancellation fee to the card on file totaling to 50% of the scheduled service(s) for appointments that are cancelled or rescheduled within 24 hours of your scheduled appointment.

Appointment Preparation Policy:

Please thoroughly remove all makeup and skin care products before arriving to your lash appointment. Eyes must be clean and oil free upon arrival. Please refrain from using eye cream, moisturizer, sunscreen, etc. prior to your appointment, as this will result in the tape and eye pads not being able to stick or stay in place. Please keep in mind, I am unable to properly adhere semi-permanent lash extensions on lashes that have make up, residue build up, or skin care products near the eye area. I want to ensure maximum bonding and retention for your lash extensions so once again, I kindly ask that you please arrive with thoroughly clean lashes and no make up or skin care products on your face.

Privacy/Social Media Policy:

Lashes By Mona is committed to protecting and respecting the privacy of its clients and users of this site. Any information Lashes By Mona collects from you is to personalize your experience, and improve customer service. All card payments and electronic transactions made with Lashes By Mona are processed through Vagaro. All social media content is approved by the client and will never be posted without consent. Here at Lashes By Mona your privacy and safety comes first, and will always remain top priority.

Late/No Show Policy:

If you are running late to your appointment, please reach out through phone call or text to prevent your appointment from being released due to the assumption that you will not show up. Every client is given a 15 minute grace period. If you are more than 15 minutes late. Please keep in mind, my appointments are booked back to back. Lashes By Mona reserves the right to charge a no show fee totaling up to 100% of the scheduled service for those who do not show up to their appointment.

FAQ

Due to different cultures and ethnic backgrounds, hormones, etc. please keep in mind that everyone has different hair growth cycles and no one is the same. Some clients may need fills every 1-2 weeks, 2-4 weeks, or 4-6 weeks. Retention is different and varies from each person. Taking very good care of your lashes and keeping them clean is promising for ensuring maximum retention.

You must have 50% of lashes remaining in order to be considered a fill. If you are missing 50% or more of your lashes, please consult with me prior to your appointment. Lash fills are eligible to book one to three weeks after your initial full set or last fill. After three weeks, a full set is required. Non negotiable.

Redness and irritation are temporary side effects to lash extensions. If you have sensitive eyes, you may experience redness, irritation, watery eyes, and burning post service. These symptoms will subside within 1-3 days and will gradually get better over time as your eyes adjust and gets used to the adhesive and having lash extensions. If symptoms persist after 3 days, please consult with me so I can evaluate the situation.

Please keep in mind during Spring and Fall when the climate changes, our lashes shed at a quicker rate and you may experience more fall out than normal. Lashes may look thinner than normal due to shedding. Also, clients that experience seasonal allergies will see quicker fall out at times due to inflammation around the eye area. Some clients may have oilier skin than others and that's totally normal, as we all have different skin types. If you happen to fall under this skin type, its highly recommended that you pat your lids dry daily to prevent quicker fall out as oil weakens the adhesive bond resulting in fall out.
